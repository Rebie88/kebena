Coronavirus (Covid-19) Resources for our community.

As COVID-19 continues to evolve across the country, the well-being of our community and members continues to be our top priority. Health officials and the government have restricted business operations, and to follow this guidance, we want to share some of the operational changes we have implemented that may affect you.

For the health and safety of our community and guests, we require all individuals in the building to wear face coverings.

As always, please remember to wash your hands before and after wearing a face covering. Prior to receiving service, one of our team members will be asking you if you are exhibiting the following symptoms:

  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Repeated shaking with chills

If the answer is YES to any of these symptoms, please contact your health care provider.

We highly recommend all our members to follow all CDC and local health care guidelines. We have a lot of volunteers to help anyone who is in need. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you need help. For more information about Covid-19 follow the link Coronavirus
