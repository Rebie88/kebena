Who We Are?

The Kebena Community is a non-profit and non-political organization. KC created by group of African immigrants who share the common goal of supporting each other. We aim to preserve our rich African heritage with the intent of passing it to the next generation. Our goal is to encourage, help each other and to create strong relationships between our community. We believe when people connect and help each other life will be enjoyable.

Have Further Question?

We are proud of our Community and invite you to share it with us.

For more information please schedule a toure or contact us today. We look forward to meeting you.

About Our Service

Wedding Services

KC provides marriage services to perform "Aqd" or "Nikah” according to Kebena tradition and culture practice of marriage principles. For our community members we have many experienced, dedicated staff will assist in planning every detail of your big day. Expect to enjoy stress-free planning and special touches such as complimentary accommodations for the bride and groom. KC cultural center can ultimately accommodate up to 250 guests and plenty of parking spaces.

Family Counseling Services

We provide cultural and spiritually based family counseling. If you visit the Kebena community family counseling center, you will find personalized, intimate programs for restoring individuals and rebuilding marriages. There are new beginnings, even for husbands and wives who think all hope is lost. We have a lot of volunteers who’s every day at Focus on the Family, we hear many types of stories—stories of relationships healed, families strengthened, and desperate situations turned around. Do not hesitate to get help.

FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE

KC gladly offers financial assistance to those who need. We simply ask that you provide us with the required information demonstrating your need for financial assistance. We are appreciative of many of you who donations. They help us to mobilize our community to provide appropriate supports during this difficult time.

OUR VALUES

Guiding us in our mission are our core values

  • Celebrate diversity in gender, age, ethnicity, religion and political viewpoint of our community members.
  • Operate and manage KC with accountability and transparency
  • Creating a welcoming environment where members learn, grow and have enjoyable experience.
  • Partnering and forging stronger relationships with other organizations to realize our vision.
  • Choosing dialogue and discussion as a way to solve problems.
